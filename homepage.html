About the Series

Algicosathlon is a groundbreaking web series that follows eight extraordinary athletes as they compete in the world's most demanding multi-sport competition. With millions on the line and personal redemption at stake, friendships will be tested, rivalries will ignite, and only the strongest will survive. Experience the raw emotion, fierce determination, and ultimate triumph in this edge-of-your-seat sports drama.
